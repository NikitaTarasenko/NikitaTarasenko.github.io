About us
Unicorn.Events - is the world largest startup pitch contest for the venture capital industry and extraordinary startups competing for the attention of the VCs and Corporations.
Unicorn Battles® take place on a regular basis online and offline in all major locations around the world.
We are able to outreach to almost any startup there is in any geographical location or industry to help our VC or Corporate partners to get the competitive advantage by contacting the best ones that currently exist in the whole world!
Whether you are an Investor looking for the future Unicorns to invest in, a Corporation looking for a solution that will ensure you outperforming the competitors, or a Startup with outstanding solution-seeking funds for the next round – JOIN UNICORN BATTLE
